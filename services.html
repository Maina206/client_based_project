Our Services

Comprehensive debt recovery and management solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of your business

Debt Recovery / Collection

This is our main area of expertise. We undertake collection services on behalf of companies and organizations that have delinquent facilities held by both retail and corporate debtors.

Our Approach

We employ multiple techniques including:

  • Client meetings
  • Issuance of letters of demand
  • Telephone negotiations
  • Legal advice and consultation

Our retail collections are customized depending on the company customer profile. We utilize field collections teams backed by call centre support to achieve high contact rates and excellent penetration levels.

Debt Classifications

Most companies classify their debts in different categories as follows:

  • The impossible (Almost written off)
  • The near impossible (May be)
  • The possible (Current)
